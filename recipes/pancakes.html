Homemade Fluffy Pancakes

There is nothing quite like enjoying a stack of warm, fluffy pancakes on a crisp winter day. There is also nothing quite like a stack of fresh pancakes on a hot summer afternoon. Truly exceptional.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 3/4 cups milk, your choice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 teaspoons of butter
    •

    Instructions

    1. Whisk the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a bowl until combined.
    2. Add the egg, milk, and vanilla. Whisk until there are no lumps, but no further.
    3. Heat your skillet of choice, adding butter so the batter doesn't stick.
    4. Pour your desired amount of batter in the skillet.
    5. When bubble begin to form and pop, the pancake is ready to be flipped.
    6. Continue making pancakes, adding more butter to the pan, every 2-3 cakes.
    7. Load up with toppings and consume.
