Naan Bread

This recipe is for easy naan bread using a cast-iron skillet. Soft and puffy, this will become a staple in your everyday cooking.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1/4 oz. active dry yeast
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Extra oil, for greasing the skillet
  • 3 tablespoons of melted butter
    •

    Instructions

    • In a bowl, combine sugar, water, and yeast. The yeast will be actvated when the mixture become foamy, about 10 minutes.
    • Take your flour and place it on a flat surface, making a well in the middle. Add the yeast mixture, yogurt, salt and oil.
    • Knead until the surface of the dough is smooth and shiny, roughly 10 minutes.
    • Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let rise in a warm place, the dough should double in size within 1 hour.
    • Divde the dough into 8 equal portions, roll each piece into a circle with a rolling pin.
    • Heat up your cast-iron skillet over high heat ad lightly grease the surface with some oil.
    • Place the dough on the skillet. When it puffs up, bubbles and has burnt spots appear, flip it over and cook the other side the same way.
    • Repeat with all pieces of dough.
    • Brush the naan with melted butter and serve warm.
